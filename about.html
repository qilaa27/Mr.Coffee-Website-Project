Our Founder

Susanto Liman

Susanto Liman’s passion for coffee began long before he founded MR.COFFEE. As a young entrepreneur, Susanto sought to create more than just a coffee shop—he envisioned a space where people could gather, relax, and share moments over exceptional coffee. What started as a dream rooted in his love for the art of coffee-making soon evolved into a thriving business dedicated to delivering both high-quality beverages and a welcoming atmosphere.

Driven by a deep commitment to creativity and community, Susanto built MR.COFFEE from the ground up, ensuring every cup served was a reflection of his personal touch and dedication to excellence. His leadership has shaped the coffee shop into a beloved destination for coffee lovers, where each visit promises not just a drink but an experience.

Today, MR.COFFEE continues to expand under Susanto's guidance, bringing people together and spreading the joy of great coffee. His vision remains clear-to inspire connections and cultivate a space where everyone feels at home, one cup at a time.

Our Mission

At MR.COFFEE, we bring the finest freshly brewed wonders to your cup, sourced from remote mountaintops where the world's best beans are grown. Our mission is to make exceptional coffee a daily pleasure, accessible to everyone, every day.

We take pride in sourcing premium beans, training talented baristas, and creating inviting spaces where our community can gather, connect, and enjoy coffee that's as extraordinary as the stories shared over it.

Through sustainable practices and ethical partnerships with coffee farmers, we aim to make a positive impact on both our local communities and the global coffee industry, one cup at a time.

Our Vision

At MR.COFFEE, we envision a world where exceptional coffee is within everyone's reach, served with care and crafted with passion.

Our goal is to become the most beloved coffee brand, not only in Indonesia but beyond, celebrated for our outstanding products and unwavering commitment to sustainability, community, and innovation.

By 2030, we aspire to establish MR.COFFEE as a global leader in the coffee industry, setting the standard for quality, accessibility, and ethical practices-one cup at a time.

Our Story

2020

MR.COFFEE was founded by Susanto Liman, driven by a passion for exceptional coffee and a vision to create a community space where people could connect over great drinks.

2022

By 2022, MR.COFFEE had established itself as a beloved destination, known for its premium beans, skilled baristas, and commitment to sustainability. The brand was growing, with Susanto's personal touch shaping every cup and building a community around it.

2023

In 2023, MR.COFFEE expanded further, continuing its mission to make exceptional coffee accessible to everyone. The brand remained dedicated to innovation, sustainability, and fostering connections both locally and globally.

2025

Today, MR.COFFEE is on track to become a global leader in the coffee industry by 2030, setting the standard for quality, sustainability, and ethical practices. Susanto's vision to create a space where everyone feels at home continues to inspire, one cup at a time.
